Pick one of the following Bingo Cards

(If you are in a group, you probably want to coordinate who has what card so you don't overlap.)

RULES

1. Everyone playing picks a bingo card!

2. Turn on some Flea Market Flip!

3. Whenever an item on your card happens, take a drink!

3a. If an item happens a second time, but for a different team or item, take another drink and keep the square marked.

3b. If marking a square results in a BINGO, then you can chug the rest of your drink.

4. Once your drink is empty, you have to play some classic flip cup!

5. The winner is whoever can successfully flip their cup first!

5a. If a bingo was achieved you must get a new card.

5a. Non-Bingoed cards can be carried over from episode to episode.
