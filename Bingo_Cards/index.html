The Story Behind DCOM Bingo

My friend and I embarked on the ultimate Disney Channel journey—we watched every single DCOM. Through this epic marathon, we conducted a thematic analysis on a large number of these beloved movies to identify common TV tropes and recurring themes that make DCOMs so wonderfully predictable.

Based on our research, we created these bingo cards to enhance your DCOM-watching experience. Each square represents a trope, character type, or situation that frequently appears across the Disney Channel Original Movie universe.

📝 Note: Some squares are biased toward older DCOMs, so feel free to adapt the nature of any square as the DCOM landscape continues to evolve!
